Frequently asked questions

What is VoxLead and how does it work?

VoxLead is a managed AI receptionist. It answers inbound calls, qualifies callers, handles simple questions, and books appointments so you don’t lose revenue to missed calls.

Will callers notice it’s AI?

Most assume it’s a human. We tune voice, phrasing, and business details during onboarding so it matches your brand and sounds natural.

Can it transfer calls to my team?

Yes. After qualification it can warm-transfer to a direct line or send notes via text/email if no one is available.

Can I control what it says?

Yes. You set hours, services, pricing notes, and rules. We maintain the script and update it as your policies change.

Does it book appointments?

Yes. We connect to your calendar (e.g., Google Calendar, Calendly, Acuity, or your practice software) and book in real time using your availability.

Will this work with my CRM or booking tool?

In most cases, yes. We integrate with popular calendars and CRMs and can use webhooks/API when needed. We confirm fit during onboarding.

Do I need to install anything?

No software to install. You forward your phone line and share calendar access. Typical setup takes ~30 minutes.

Which businesses get the most value?

Clinics and service businesses that rely on phone bookings: dental, med spa, chiropractic, legal, home services, real estate, and more.

What does it cost and are there contracts?

$499 covers setup and your first month. After installation, $199/mo starts in month two. Month-to-month, cancel anytime.
