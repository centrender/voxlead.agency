Privacy Policy — VoxLead.Agency

Last updated: November 9, 2025

Plain-English summary below. This page covers website, demo forms, calls, and SMS handled by our AI receptionist system.

What we do

VoxLead.Agency provides AI-powered appointment and messaging automation for service businesses. We collect only the data needed to book, confirm, reschedule, or follow up on appointments and demos.

Information we collect

  • Contact info — name, phone, email submitted during bookings or demos.
  • Business info — clinic or business name, city, niche, and preferences for setup.
  • Communication data — call/SMS metadata and transcripts when routed via providers like Twilio.
  • Technical — basic device and site analytics (non-sensitive) to improve reliability.

How we use it

  • Send confirmations, reminders, and updates.
  • Route calls and messages to calendars or staff as configured.
  • Improve system accuracy and service quality.

No sale of data. We never sell or share data with third parties for marketing.

Legal basis & consent

By submitting your details for a booking or demo, you consent to receive related messages. Businesses using VoxLead must ensure their use complies with local laws and consent rules.

SMS terms

  • Message & data rates may apply.
  • Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for help.
  • Message frequency varies with bookings and follow-ups.

Data retention

We keep data only as long as needed for service operations, troubleshooting, and legal requirements, then delete or de-identify it securely.

Security

Data is stored with reputable providers and protected by access controls. No system can be 100% secure, but we apply industry-standard safeguards.

Third-party processors

We use vendors such as Twilio (telephony/SMS) and Google (calendar/forms). Processing follows each vendor’s terms and privacy policies.

Your rights

  • Access, correction, or deletion of your data where applicable.
  • Opt-out of SMS anytime by replying STOP.
